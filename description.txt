Girlfriend Mod lets you befriend and romance the Lost Girl found in caves!

Features:
- Send specific emotes to befriend the Lost Girl instead of fighting her
- Progress through different relationship stages with dialogue options
- Give her a diamond ring to make her your girlfriend
- Let her move into a house on the surface
- She'll learn to cook for you if you place a cooking pot in her house!

How to befriend the Lost Girl:
1. Find a Lost Girl in caves
2. Use emotes (right-click empty slot at bottom-right of inventory)
3. Send the sequence: Heart, Happy, Wave, Question

The mod integrates with Terraria's emote system - no custom UI needed!
